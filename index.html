You know the drill: you’re off on another trip and either the hotel gym sucks, or it doesn’t even have one. What other options do you have? You could call up all the local gyms and ask for a day pass, but would you really be happy paying 10€, 20€ or even 35€ for a single day pass? These are clearly designed to convert visitors into regular members to get a better rate. But what about the travellers who can’t sign a contract, like you and me?

How it works? Simple! You buy a Gymtogo membership card, right here on this website. Then when you’re in a foreign city and need to get that workout in, you simply check for the nearest partner gym and show them your card. You will be granted access, free of charge.
